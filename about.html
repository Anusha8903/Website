Grilled Mediterranean Sea Bass

This dish features a perfectly grilled Mediterranean Sea Bass, known for its delicate flavor and flaky texture. It's paired with a vibrant, herb-infused olive oil sauce, topped with roasted cherry tomatoes and fresh arugula. The combination of the smoky grill, the richness of the fish, and the tangy, fresh garnishes makes for a mouthwatering experience that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine.

Served with a side of roasted vegetables and crispy potatoes, this dish is a celebration of fresh, seasonal ingredients and classic grilling techniques.

Truffle Mushroom Risotto

Our Truffle Mushroom Risotto is the epitome of comfort and luxury. This creamy, rich dish is made with the finest Arborio rice, slowly cooked to perfection with a savory mushroom stock and finished with a touch of white truffle oil. The earthy flavor of the mushrooms blends harmoniously with the subtle fragrance of the truffle, creating a dish that is both indulgent and satisfying.

Topped with freshly grated Parmesan and a sprinkle of fresh herbs, this risotto is a true comfort food with a gourmet twist, ideal for any occasion.
